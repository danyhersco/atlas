Cash flow refers to the movement of money in and out of your hands. Managing it wisely is crucial to avoid shortages and ensure you can meet all financial obligations.

### Why It Matters

- Prevents overspending
- Ensures bills are paid on time
- Allows for savings and investment
- Reduces financial stress

### Strategies

1. **Track Your Spending:** Use apps or a manual journal.
2. **Prioritize Regular Expenses:** Automate payments if possible.
3. **Build a Buffer:** Maintain an emergency fund (typically 3-6 months' worth of expenses).
4. **Delay Discretionary Purchases:** Wait before making non-essential purchases.
5. **Review Regularly:** Adjust budget based on changing circumstances.
