This course, "Principles of Financial Literacy," is designed to equip you with the essential knowledge and tools to make sound financial decisions. By the end of the course, you will be able to:

1. Understand and apply basic financial concepts in your personal and professional life.
2. Develop practical skills in budgeting, saving, investing, and managing credit.
3. Interpret financial information and documentation.
4. Recognize and avoid common financial mistakes and pitfalls.
5. Lay the groundwork for further study in finance, business, or economics.
